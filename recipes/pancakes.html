Blueberry Pancakes

About the Dish

Pancakes are a traditional breakfast treat that are easy to make. They are a type of flatbread that is made from a batter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan. The batter is made from flour, eggs, milk, and butter. Pancakes are often served with syrup, butter, or fruit. They can also be filled with sweet or savory fillings such as blueberries, chocolate chips, or cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of flour
  • 2 Tablespoon of granulated sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 Cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of vegatable oil, divided
  • 2 1/4 Cups of fresh blueberries, cut in halves
  • 1/2 cup of cold whipping cream or already prepped whipped cream
